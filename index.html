Projects

TacOMG!

TacOMG is a web application where a user can log in using their Facebook credentials and create their own taco, post their taco, and view their taco and other user's tacos in the gallery. Technologies used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.

DroneDB

A web application for people who are new to First Person View (FPV) Drone Racing. The app walks the user through the parts they will need to build a drone of their very own, with links to buy individual parts. Technologies used: HTML, CSS, Materialize, React, Express, Node.js, and MongoDB.

Desk Of

Desk Of is an interactive social website where users can design their desks and view the desks of others, while hunting for hidden treasures and earning more junk to clutter their own desks. Technologies used: HTML, CSS, jQuery, Python, Django.
